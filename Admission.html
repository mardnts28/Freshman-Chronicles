Admission Requirements

Senior High School
  • Original Junior High School Report Card (Form 138, now known as SF9)
  • 2 pcs 2" x 2" recent ID pictures
  • PSA Birth Certificate (Original Copy)
  • Certificate of Good Moral Character
  • DepEd Voucher Certification (if applicable)
  • Certificate of Honors (for SHS Graduate with honors)

For transferees, submit an Original Copy of Transfer Credentials alongside rest of requirements.

Note: T.I.P. Manila no longer offers Senior High School (SHS). Applicants who wish to pursue their SHS education with T.I.P. may consider enrolling at our Quezon City Campus.

Transferees
  • Original High School Report Card (Form 138/SF9) in Grade 12
  • PSA Birth Certificate (Original Copy)
  • Certificate of Good Moral Character
  • Two (2) copies of 2" x 2" recent ID pictures
  • Certificate of Honors (for SHS graduates with honors)
  • Filipino Graduates of Secondary Education from Philippine Secondary Schools Abroad
    • High School Report Card (Form 138/SF9) in Grade 12 or Certificate of Completion of Secondary Education or its equivalent
    • Philippine Statistics Authority (PSA) Birth Certificate
    • Certificate of Good Moral Character (with school seal)
    • Two (2) copies of 2"x 2" recent picture
    • Photocopy of Philippine passport
    • Certificate of Honors / Rank, if applicable
  • Filipino Graduates of Secondary Education from Foreign Schools
    • Authenticated Scholastic Records and Certification of Graduation or Certification of Completion of Secondary Education or its equivalent
    • Certification of Equivalency from DepEd
    • Philippine Statistics Authority (PSA) Birth Certificate
    • Two (2) copies of 2"x 2" recent picture
    • Original and photocopy of Philippine passport / Foreign Passport
    • Authenticated Identification Certificate for Dual Citizenship under RA 9225, if holder of foreign passport
  • Filipino graduates prior to S.Y. 2014-2014 who did not enroll in any tertiary institution immediately after graduation
    • High School Report Card (Form 138)
    • Philippine Statistics Authority (PSA) Birth Certificate
    • Certification from the high school principal stating that the F137- A has not been forwarded to any tertiary institution / Affidavit of non enrollment in other HEls.
    • Certificate of Good Moral Character (with school seal)
    • Four (4) copies of 2"x 2" recent picture
    • Certificate of Honors / Rank, if applicable
  • Filipino applicants with PEPT/ALS Certificates
    • Examinee Report Form (ERF)/Certificate of Rating in Secondary Level
    • Completion Certificate for Secondary Level
    • Philippine Statistics Authority (PSA) Birth Certificate
    • Authenticated Diploma in Secondary Education Equivalency
    • Four (4) copies of 2"x 2" recent picture
  • Transferees / Second Degree Applicants / Graduates of Associate Programs:
    • Original Transfer Credentials / Honor Dismissal Official Transcript of Records / True Copy of Grades from the last school attended Certificate of Good Moral Character Course Descriptions Philippine Statistics Authority (PSA) Birth Certificate Two (2) copies 2"x 2" recent picture
  • Cross-Enrollees
    • Permit to Cross Enroll from their home school signed by the Registrar Two (2) copies of 2"x 2" recent picture
Graduate Students
  • Transfer Credentials/Honorable Dismissal
  • Original Transcript of Records or True copy of grades from the last school attended
  • Curriculum Vitae (CV)
  • Original Philippine Statistics Authority (PSA) Birth Certificate
  • 2pcs identical 2" x 2" recent ID pictures
  • 2 recommendation letters from the immediate superior
