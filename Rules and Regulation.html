RULES AND REGULATIONS IN LIBRARY

    Silence must be observed at all times.

    Sleeping, eating, drinking, smoking, and other disruptive behavior are strictly prohibited inside the T.I.P. Libraries and shall be subject to disciplinary action.

    Library users should observe courtesy at all times: Any act of discourtesy toward the librarians shall be duly noted for appropriate action.

    All materials, resources, facilities, and utilities of the library should be used with proper care.

    utilating, defacing, tearing, and vandalizing of books or other properties of the library are strictly prohibited and subject to disciplinary action and suspension of library privileges.

    Library users must prominently display their Student ID on them at all times: Students who fail to do so will not be allowed access to the Library facilities.

    No items belonging to the library are to be taken out of the library unless they have been checked out at the circulation desk and at the security post.

    Mobile phones or any other personal electronic gadgets must be switched to silent mode before entering the library.

    Making unreasonable noise, loud conversations, loud cell phone calls, or playing loud music or video that can distract other library users in the library is not permitted.

    Users are prohibited from making or answering calls within the quiet zone.

    The discussion room is used for academic purposes only and should not be used for private study or social purposes.

    Library furniture/equipment should not be moved from its original location.

    Library users should deposit bags and plastic envelopes /or folders at the baggage counter before entering the library. Valuable things (calculator, wallet or coin purse, laptop, cellular phones, etc.) must be taken inside the premises.

    Personal belongings should not be left unattended. The library management will not be held responsible for the loss of personal belongings.

    When a user is caught for breaching the library rules and regulations, immediate action will be taken.
