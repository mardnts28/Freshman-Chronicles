ONLINE PLATFORMS

CANVAS:

Canvas is an online-based learning management system (LMS) that helps schools, instructors, and learners access, arrange, and collaborate on course content while keeping track of pupil performance and skill improvements.

BIG BLUE BUTTON:

Big Blue Button is an online feature that can be seen and used through the installation of Canvas. This feature is used for online classes and asynchronous activities which can really help the students and teachers for continuous interactive learning.

ARIS PORTAL:

The TIP Aris portal is an online platform where updates, grades, and the total evaluation of your grades can be seen. Additionally, the tracks of your previous semester, medical records, and even the fees are also being recorded, which can be seen on this online platform.
